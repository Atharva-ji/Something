Interesting Facts about IPL

Ordered List of Key Facts

  1. Chennai Super Kings has the highest winning ratio at 60.42% in IPL history.
  2. Mumbai Indians hold the record for the most wins by any team in IPL, with 91 victories.
  3. Royal Challengers Bangalore has the record for both the highest (263) and lowest (49) scores in a IPL match.
  4. Chris Gayle holds the record for the most sixes hit in IPL history, with 292 sixes.

Unordered List of Additional Facts

  • Dale Steyn recorded the most dot balls in a single IPL season, with 212 dot balls in 2013.
  • Kolkata Knight Riders made a record of nine consecutive wins in a single season including the finals(2014).
  • There have never been two consecutive finals since 2008 without M.S.Dhoni .
  • Sandeep Lamichhane is the first Nepalese cricketer to play in the IPL.
